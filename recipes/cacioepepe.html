Cacio E Pepe

Description

Cacio e pepe. Another one of the five Roman classic pastas. This is perhaps the simplest of the dishes and the most cherished. Consisting of pecorino romano cheese and black pepper, this dish only has three ingredients when you add in the pasta. It is one of my personal favorites and one that I like to bastardize by using the same method on my oatmeal.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti. 16 oz.
  • Black Pepper. 3 tbsp.
  • Pecorino Romano Cheese. 8 oz.

Steps

  1. Boil water in pot. Throw in pasta to cook.
  2. Freshly grind 3 tbsp worth of black pepper and grate cheese.
  3. When pasta is finished, strain, reserving 1 cup of water.
  4. In the now drained pasta pot, add the pasta, water, cheese, and pepper. Stir vigorouly until emuslified.
  5. Serve and enjoy.
