Pasta alla Gricia

Description

Pasta alla Gricia. It can be thought of as a diet carbonara as it skips out on using the egg. That said it's still a very filling dish that has a wonderful flavor profile of its own. It's another one of the four Roman pastas and an absolute MUST try.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti. 12 oz.
  • Guanciale. 4 oz.
  • Pecorino Romano Cheese. 8 oz.

Steps

  1. Boil water and toss pasta in.
  2. In another pan, brown guanciale
  3. Put pasta in guanciale pan with water and cheese.
  4. Stir vigorouly until emuslified.
  5. Serve and enjoy.
